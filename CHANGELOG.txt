Nano GDPR - Changelog

1.0.0 - 2026-06-19
  First release.
  - Cookie consent banner and preferences modal.
  - Saves consent in the browser only (localStorage + cookie). No server, no database.
  - Gates analytics/marketing scripts until consent is given.
  - "Cookie settings" link to reopen and change choices.
  - One-snippet install. No build step. No dependencies.
  - Optional logging (optional-logging folder) for proof of consent. Needs PHP. Off by default.
  - MIT licence. Free. No footer link.
